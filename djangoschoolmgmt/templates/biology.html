Biology Department

Greenwood University

Bachelor's Course Structure

  • Duration: 3-4 years (depending on the program structure).

  • Semester Start: Usually in August or September.
  • Introduction to Biology
  • Genetics and Molecular Biology
  • Ecology and Environmental Science
  • Cell Biology
  • Evolutionary Biology
  • Physiology
  • Evolutionary Biology
  • Biochemistry
  • Microbiology
  • Marine Biology
  • Biotechnology

Master's Course Structure

  • Duration: 1-2 years (Masters) or 3-5 years (Ph.D.).

  • Semester Start: Typically in August or September for Masters programs; Ph.D. programs may have rolling admissions.
  • Advanced Cell Biology
  • Genomics and Proteomics
  • Bioinformatics
  • Biostatistics
  • Immunology
  • Neurobiology
  • Environmental Biology

Diploma Course Structure

  • Duration: 1-2 years (Masters) or 3-5 years (Ph.D.).

  • Semester Start: Typically in August or September for Masters programs; Ph.D. programs may have rolling admissions.
  • Advanced Research Topics in Biology
  • Dissertation Proposal
  • Specialized Topics in Molecular Biology
  • Ethics in Biological Research
  • Teaching Practicum
  • Dissertation Research and Writing

Doctorate (Ph.D) Course Structure

  • Duration: Typically 3-5 years (may vary based on research and dissertation work).
  • Semester Start: Rolling admissions, often with ongoing research projects.
  • Advanced Research Methodologies
  • Literature Review in Biology
  • Advanced Molecular Techniques
  • Grant Writing and Funding
  • Specialized Seminars in Biology Topics
  • Dissertation Research and Writing

Foundation Course Structure

  • Duration: Varies based on the program, typically 1 year.
  • Semester Start: Generally aligned with undergraduate intake, often in August or September.
  • Introduction to Biology Concepts
  • Basic Chemistry and Physics for Biology
  • Mathematics for Biology
  • Introduction to Scientific Research
  • Fundamentals of Genetics
  • Ecology and Environmental Science Basics
  • FLaboratory Skills and Techniques
  • Introduction to Bioinformatics

Scholarships and Financial Aid

Greenwood University offers scholarships and financial aid programs for eligible Biology students. Reach out to university for more details.
