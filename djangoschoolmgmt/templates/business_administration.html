Business Administration Department

Greenwood University

Bachelor's Course Structure

  • Duration: 3-4 years (depending on the program structure).

  • Semester Start: Usually in August or September.
  • Introduction to Business Administration
  • Principles of Management
  • Business Ethics and Corporate Social Responsibility
  • Marketing Management
  • Financial Management
  • Operations Management
  • Strategic Management
  • Entrepreneurship
  • Human Resource Management
  • International Business
  • Business Analytics

Master's Course Structure

  • Duration: 1-2 years (Masters) or 3-5 years (Ph.D.).

  • Semester Start: Typically in August or September for Masters programs; Ph.D. programs may have rolling admissions.
  • Advanced Business Strategy
  • Business Leadership and Change Management
  • Business Analytics and Decision Making
  • Financial Reporting and Analysis
  • Global Business Environment
  • Marketing Strategy
  • Supply Chain Management

Diploma Course Structure

  • Duration: 1-2 years (Masters) or 3-5 years (Ph.D.).

  • Semester Start: Typically in August or September for Masters programs; Ph.D. programs may have rolling admissions.
  • Advanced Business Research Topics
  • Dissertation Proposal in Business Administration
  • Specialized Topics in Business Management
  • Ethics in Business Research
  • Teaching Practicum in Business
  • Dissertation Research and Writing in Business Administration

Doctorate (Ph.D) Course Structure

  • Duration: Typically 3-5 years (may vary based on research and dissertation work).
  • Semester Start: Rolling admissions, often with ongoing research projects.
  • Advanced Research Methodologies in Business
  • Literature Review in Business Administration
  • Advanced Data Analysis Techniques in Business
  • Grant Writing and Funding in Business Research
  • Specialized Seminars in Business Topics
  • Dissertation Research and Writing in Business

Foundation Course Structure

  • Duration: Varies based on the program, typically 1 year.
  • Semester Start: Generally aligned with undergraduate intake, often in August or September.
  • Introduction to Business Concepts
  • Basic Economics for Business
  • Mathematics for Business
  • Introduction to Business Research
  • Fundamentals of Management
  • Business Environment Analysis
  • Laboratory Skills and Techniques for Business
  • Introduction to Business Analytics

Scholarships and Financial Aid

Greenwood University offers scholarships and financial aid programs for eligible Business Administration students. Reach out to the university for more details.
